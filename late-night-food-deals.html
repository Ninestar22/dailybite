Late-night rules of thumb

  • Drive-thru outlives the lobby. After ~10pm assume dining rooms are closed; the drive-thru line is the real schedule.
  • Order in the app before you get in line. Deals apply automatically and you skip the drive-thru menu-stare.
  • Check the app for hours, not the door. Chains update app hours faster than signage.
  • Weekends run later. Fri/Sat hours are typically 1–2 hours longer than weeknights.
