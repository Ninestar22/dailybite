Move-in week is the most expensive food month of the year — you don't have a kitchen yet, the dining plan hasn't kicked in, and everything is takeout. Here's every student food deal worth claiming before classes start, all verified for the 2026 back-to-school season.

Set these up before move-in day

Two things unlock most student food savings, and both take five minutes with your .edu email:

  • Chipotle U Rewards — enroll and verify as a student for a 1,000-point bonus (real progress toward a free entrée) and 20% faster points earning. Chipotle also runs free-guac and free-drink student promos in the fall.
  • Grubhub+ Student — free Grubhub+ membership for verified students means $0 delivery fees on eligible campus orders. If you order delivery even twice a month, this pays for itself instantly.
