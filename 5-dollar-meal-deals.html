Verdicts

Most food per dollar: Taco Bell

Five items including two full entrées. If sheer volume is the goal, the Luxe Box wins and it isn't close — the Chalupa alone is a $4+ item.

Best burger-meal replacement: Wendy's

The Biggie Bag is the only one that doesn't force the app, the Jr. Bacon Cheeseburger is the best sandwich in any $5 box, and Wendy's lets you stack an offer and a points reward on the same order.

Best if you're already in the app: McDonald's

Functionally identical to the Biggie Bag, and app-only. The real McDonald's move is pairing the box with the deals tab — free-fries offers frequently stack the same visit.

Best breakfast play: Dunkin'

Coffee plus two sandwiches for $6 is the strongest morning value on this list — a Starbucks drink alone costs more.

The math nobody does

Ordering any of these à la carte runs $9–$13. Buying the box instead saves roughly $4–$7 per meal — do that four times a week and you're keeping over $100/month. That's the entire thesis of this site.
