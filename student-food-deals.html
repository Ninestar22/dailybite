Do chains have actual student discounts?

A few do (Chick-fil-A, Subway, Burger King at participating locations, usually 10% with student ID) — but they're franchise-by-franchise and rarely stack with app deals. The short answer: app deals beat student discounts almost every time. Ask at your local spot once, then default to the apps.
