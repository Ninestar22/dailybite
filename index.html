Questions, answered

Everything you'd want to know before trusting a deals site.

How often are deals updated?

Every morning, seven days a week. An automated check runs across 30+ national chains, expired offers are dropped, and everything on the page is re-verified against official sources before it goes live.

Are these deals actually real?

Yes — every deal links to the restaurant's own app or official page, and each one is re-checked daily. If we can't confirm a deal is still active, it comes off the list the same morning.

Do I need to sign up for anything?

No. DailyBite is free, there's no account, and we only list deals anyone can claim — no first-order-only promos, no loyalty-point games, no paid memberships.

Why are so many deals "app only"?

Restaurant chains put their best prices inside their own apps. The apps are free to download, and we tell you exactly which app each deal lives in.

How does DailyBite make money?

Some links may be affiliate links, meaning we can earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. It never affects which deals get listed — verified value is the only criterion.
