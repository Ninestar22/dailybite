About DailyBite

DailyBite is a free, daily-updated guide to the best fast-food and coffee-chain app deals in the United States. Every morning, we re-check current offers from major national chains — McDonald's, Taco Bell, Wendy's, Starbucks, Domino's, Chipotle, and a dozen more — against official brand sources and reputable deal trackers, then publish only the deals we can verify.

How deals are verified

Our automated research pipeline runs every day at roughly 7:00 AM Eastern. It searches official brand deal pages and established deal-tracking publications, discards anything it cannot confirm as currently active, and rebuilds every page on this site. If a deal can't be verified, it doesn't get published — simple as that. Offers can still vary by location, so always confirm in the restaurant's own app before ordering.

Why we built this

Food prices keep climbing, and the best discounts now hide inside a dozen different restaurant apps. DailyBite pulls them into one page so you can check once each morning and eat cheaper. A companion mobile app is in the works.

How the site makes money

Some links on DailyBite are affiliate links — if you sign up for a service or place an order through them, we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you. That's what keeps the site free. See our Privacy & Disclosures page for details.
