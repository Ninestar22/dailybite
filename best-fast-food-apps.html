Why each app earns its spot

1. Whataburger

Sign-up freebie: Free Whataburger just for joining. Ongoing: Points on every purchase. The single best no-strings sign-up freebie in fast food right now.

2. Taco Bell

Sign-up freebie: Free Cantina Chicken Crispy Taco, Beefy 5-Layer Burrito, or Soft Taco on signup. Ongoing: New deals drop every Tuesday; occasional $10 Taco Pass (a taco a day for a month). Best recurring deal engine — the Tuesday drops are consistently strong.

3. Wendy's

Sign-up freebie: Free 10-pc nuggets offer + 150 bonus points with first purchase. Ongoing: 10 pts/$1; 200 pts = free nuggets, fries, or Frosty; offers stack with rewards in one order. 150 signup points alone already cover a free Frosty.

4. Einstein Bros.

Sign-up freebie: Free bagel & shmear on signup. Ongoing: 2 pts/$1 across Einstein/Bruegger's/Noah's; $9 baker's dozen Mondays. Sneaky good — plus a free egg sandwich every birthday.

5. Chipotle

Sign-up freebie: Free guac on signup. Ongoing: 10 pts/$1 toward free extras and entrées. Free guac is ~$3 — the rewards curve after that is slow but real.

6. McDonald's

Sign-up freebie: Free fries/sandwich offer with $1 first purchase. Ongoing: 100 pts/$1; frequent $2–$5 app-only bundles like the $5 Value Meal Box. The deals tab beats the rewards — check it before every order.

7. Jimmy John's

Sign-up freebie: Free sandwich after first order. Ongoing: Badges and surprise offers. An $8–$12 sandwich free after order #1 is a top-3 signup value.

8. Dunkin'

Sign-up freebie: —. Ongoing: 10 pts/$1; freebies start at just $15 of spend; triple points around your birthday. Fastest points-to-free-food curve of the coffee apps.

9. Burger King

Sign-up freebie: —. Ongoing: 10 Crowns/$1; free items from ~$25 spend; free size upgrades; 2x Crowns birthday month. Weakest signup bonus here, but the everyday app coupons run deep.

10. Sonic

Sign-up freebie: —. Ongoing: 50% off drinks & slushes on every single app order. Not a signup bonus — a permanent discount. If you drink Sonic, this pays forever.

The cashback stack: get paid on top of every deal

Chain apps give you deals; these apps pay you on top of them. Stack both on the same order:

  • Upside — cashback at participating fast food locations (and gas). Claim the offer in-app before you pay, snap the receipt.
  • Fetch — scan any restaurant receipt for points; redeem for gift cards. Zero effort floor.
  • Ibotta — rotating restaurant and grocery offers; best when a bonus multiplier hits fast food.

A $5 Biggie Bag ordered through Wendy's app, paid with a discounted gift card, receipt scanned into Fetch — that's three layers of savings on one meal.

If you only install three

Broke-student loadout: Taco Bell (best recurring deals), McDonald's (best bundles), and one cashback app (Upside or Fetch). Grab the one-time signup freebies from the rest whenever you're nearby — there's no rule saying you have to keep them installed.
